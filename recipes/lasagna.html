Traditional Lasagna

Stacked layers of delicious mince, creamy cheese, and authentic pasta? Can't wait! In this recipe,
we will teach you how to make a delicious, traditional, Italian-style lasagna!


Ensure you have all ingredients listed. We don't want any extra trips to the store!


Ingredients

You are more than welcome to customise these ingredients to your liking. However, if this is your
first time, we recommend following the ingredients and amounts provided for a delicious dinner.

  • 1 Pound Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4 Pound Lean Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Cup Minced Onion
  • 2 Garlic Cloves Crushed
  • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 Cans of Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Cans of Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • 2 Tablespoons White Sugar
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 Egg
  • Cheeses:
    • 3/4 Cup Grated Parmesan
    • 3/4 Pound Mozzarella Sliced
    • 16 Ounces Ricotta
  • Spices:
    • 4 Tablespoons Chopped Fresh Parsley
    • 1 and 1/2 Teaspoons Dried Basil Leaves
    • 1 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
    • 1/2 Teaspoon Fennel Seeds
    • 1 and 1/2 Teaspoons Salt
    • 1/4 Teaspoon Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well
    browned.
  2. Stir in crush tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons
    pasrley, basil leaves, 1 teaspoon salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer,
    simmer, covered for about 1 and 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water
    for 8 to 10 minutes, or until soft. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  6. To assemble, spread 1 and 1/12 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta mixture.
    Top with 1/3 of the mozzzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 and 1/2 cups meat over mozzarella,
    and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to
    prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  9. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before dividing and serving.
