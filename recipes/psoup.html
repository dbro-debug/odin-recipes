Pumpkin Soup

Creamy Pumpkin Soup

Warm your belly and your heart with this delicious, steamy pumpkin soup recipe with only 15
minutes of prep time, then let it cook for an hour while your house smells more and more like fall.


Ensure you have all 9 ingredients listed. We don't want any extra trips to the store!


Ingredients

You are more than welcome to customise these ingredients to your liking. However, if this is your
first time, we recommend following the ingredients and amounts provided for a delicious soup.

  • 6 Cups Chicken Stock
  • 4 Cups Pumpkin Puree
  • 1 Cup Chopped Onion
  • 1 Garlic Clove, Minced
  • 1/2 Cup Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1 and 1/2 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Chopped Fresh Thyme
  • 5 Whole Blakc Peppercorns
  • 1 Teaspoon Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Incorperate chicken stock, pumpkin, onion, garlic, salt, thyme, and peppercorns into a large
    pot; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer, uncovered for 30 minutes.
  2. Transfer soup to a blender or food processor in batches; blend until smooth. Return
    soup to the pot and bring to a boil; reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes, uncovered.
  3. Stir in heavy cream.
  4. Ladle soup into serving bowls and garnish with fresh parsley.
